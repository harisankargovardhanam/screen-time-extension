Welcome to Screen Time

Know where your time goes. Set limits. Stay focused.
Everything stays on your device — nothing is ever uploaded.

1

Pin the extension

Click the puzzle icon 🧩 in the toolbar and pin Screen Time. The badge shows live time on the current site.

2

Set your first limit

Open the popup, type a site like youtube.com and a daily budget in minutes. Turn on the shield to hard-block the site when time runs out.

3

Try a focus session

One click blocks all your limited sites for 25 or 50 minutes. The badge counts down; a notification celebrates when you finish.

4

Check your insights

The bar-chart button opens a full dashboard: daily graphs, categories, a productivity score, hour-by-hour heatmap and CSV/JSON export.
